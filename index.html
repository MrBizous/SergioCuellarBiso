Politics in Graphic Design of the 20th Century

The 20th century was a period of immense political and artistic change, and graphic design played a crucial role in shaping public opinion. From Soviet constructivist propaganda to the counterculture posters of the 1960s, design was a powerful tool of communication.

In this blog, we will explore the intersections of politics and graphic design, examining key movements, influential designers, and the impact of visual storytelling in political discourse.
